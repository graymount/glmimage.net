## Introduction

Welcome to **GLM Image** (glmimage.net). GLM Image is an AI image generation comparison platform that lets you compare outputs from multiple AI models side by side. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our service.

**Effective Date**: February 6, 2026

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information
- **What**: Email address, name, and profile information (including data from third-party login providers such as Google).
- **Why**: To create and manage your account, authenticate your identity, and provide customer support.

### 2. Usage Data
- **What**: Prompts you submit, images generated, models selected, generation history, credit usage, and interaction patterns.
- **Why**: To provide the image generation service, track credit consumption, and improve our product.

### 3. Payment Information
- **What**: Payment method details (credit card, etc.), billing address, and transaction history.
- **Why**: To process payments for subscriptions and credit purchases. Payment information is processed directly by **Stripe** and is never stored on our servers. See [Stripe's Privacy Policy](https://stripe.com/privacy).

### 4. Device and Technical Data
- **What**: IP address, browser type, operating system, device type, and access timestamps.
- **Why**: To ensure service security, prevent abuse, and optimize performance.

### 5. Cookies
- **What**: Session cookies and authentication tokens.
- **Why**: To maintain your login session and remember your preferences. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve the GLM Image service
- Process image generation requests across multiple AI models
- Manage your account and credit balance
- Process payments and subscriptions
- Send service-related notifications (account, billing, usage alerts)
- Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
- Comply with legal obligations

We do **not** use your information for:
- Selling to third parties
- Targeted advertising
- Training our own AI models with your prompts or generated images

## Third-Party AI Model Providers

GLM Image sends your prompts to the following third-party AI model providers to generate images:

- **Fal.ai** — Flux Pro, Flux Dev, Recraft, Ideogram models
- **Google** — Gemini image generation model

When you submit a prompt, it is transmitted to these providers for processing. Each provider has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data:
- [Fal.ai Privacy Policy](https://fal.ai/privacy)
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

We do not control how these providers process your prompts after transmission. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored in databases hosted on **Supabase** (cloud infrastructure).
- Generated images are stored temporarily and may expire after a period of time.
- We use encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and implement access controls to protect your data.
- No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Data Retention

- **Account data**: Retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.
- **Generation history**: Retained for service functionality and credit tracking.
- **Payment records**: Retained as required by applicable tax and financial regulations.

## Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate personal data
- **Delete** your personal data
- **Export** your data in a portable format
- **Opt out** of non-essential data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email address below.

### California Residents (CCPA)
California residents have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to request deletion. We do not sell personal information.

### EU/EEA Residents (GDPR)
If you are in the EU/EEA, our legal basis for processing your data includes: performance of a contract (providing the service), legitimate interests (security, fraud prevention), and consent (where applicable).

## Children's Privacy

GLM Image is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on this page and updating the effective date. Your continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
